This file contains the info for items needed to unlock doomtrain.

There is 3 entries of 4 bytes.

| Entry | Bytes (hex) | Item ID (dec) | Quantity | Item Name                                                               |
|-------|-------------|---------------|----------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0     | 42 06 00 00 | 66            | 6        | [Steel Pipe]({{site.baseurl}}/technical-reference/list/item-list)       |
| 1     | 88 06 00 00 | 136           | 6        | [Malboro Tentacle]({{site.baseurl}}/technical-reference/list/item-list) |
| 2     | 11 06 00 00 | 17            | 6        | [Remedy+]({{site.baseurl}}/technical-reference/list/item-list)          |

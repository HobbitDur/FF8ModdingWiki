-   Opcode: **0x0C4**
-   Short name: **ALLSEVOLTRANS**
-   Long name: Transition Volume of all Sound Effects

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Frame count*

*Final Volume (0-127)*

**ALLSEVOLTRANS**

#### Description

Transitions the volume of all sound effects.

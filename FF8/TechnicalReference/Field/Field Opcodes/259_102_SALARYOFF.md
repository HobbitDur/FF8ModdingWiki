-   Opcode: **0x102**
-   Short name: **SALARYOFF** (exe symbol; historically misspelled SARALYOFF)
-   Long name: Salary off

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

none

#### Description

Disables the payment of salaries.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_SALARYOFF` at 0x51DB90.

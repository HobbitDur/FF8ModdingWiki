-   Opcode: **0x168**
-   Short name: **UNKNOWN3**
-   Long name: Head pose (look up)

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Direction (0 = straight, 1 = right, 2 = left)*

**UNKNOWN3**

#### Description

Same as [UNKNOWN2](../167-unknown2/) but with the pitch negated (−207): rotates the entity's head joint to an upward-looking pose over 30 frames, with an optional sideways component of ±32.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_UNKNOWN3` at 0x529F70 (renamed `SCRIPT_UNKNOWN3_head_pose_up` in the community IDB).

-   Opcode: **0x055**
-   Short name: **PDIRA**
-   Long name: Direct to Party Actor

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Party slot* (0-2)

**PDIRA**

#### Description

Pops one value (a party slot 0-2), resolves it to the field entity currently assigned to that party slot (via the savemap party-entity map), and instantly sets this entity's facing (+577) to the angle toward that party member's position. Same as [DIRA](../054-dira/) but targeting a party member by slot instead of an actor ID. Instant, non-blocking.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_PDIRA` at 0x526F70.

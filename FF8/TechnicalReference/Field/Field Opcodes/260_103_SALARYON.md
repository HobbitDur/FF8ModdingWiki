-   Opcode: **0x103**
-   Short name: **SALARYON** (exe symbol; historically misspelled SARALYON)
-   Long name: Salary on

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

none

#### Description

Enables the payment of salaries.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_SALARYON` at 0x51DBC0.

-   Opcode: **0x040**
-   Short name: **PMOVEA**
-   Long name: Move to Party Actor

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Party member ID*

*Distance to stop*

**PMOVEA**

#### Description

Moves this entity towards the party member with the given ID (0, 1, or 2).

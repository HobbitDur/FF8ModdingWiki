-   Opcode: **0x105**
-   Short name: **SALARYDISPON** (exe symbol; historically misspelled SARALYDISPON)
-   Long name: Salary display on

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

none

#### Description

Turns on the display of salary alerts.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_SALARYDISPON` at 0x51DC10.

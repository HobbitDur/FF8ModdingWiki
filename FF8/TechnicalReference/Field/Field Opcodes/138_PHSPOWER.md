-   Opcode: **0x138**
-   Short name: **PHSPOWER**
-   Long name: Enable/Disable PHS

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*0 or 1 (0=disable, 1=enable)*

**PHSPOWER**

#### Description

Enables or disables the ability to switch party members.

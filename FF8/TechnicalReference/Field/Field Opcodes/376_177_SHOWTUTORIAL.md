-   Opcode: **0x177**
-   Short name: **SHOWTUTORIAL**
-   Long name: Show tutorial

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Tutorial ID*

**SHOWTUTORIAL**

#### Description

Shows a tutorial.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_SHOWTUTORIAL` at 0x522030. This is the **last opcode of the PC table** (376 entries, 0x000-0x177).

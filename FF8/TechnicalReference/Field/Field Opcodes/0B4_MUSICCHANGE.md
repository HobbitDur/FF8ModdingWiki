-   Opcode: **0x0B4**
-   Short name: **MUSICCHANGE**
-   Long name: Start music

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

none

#### Description

Stops the currently playing background music and starts playing music loaded by [MUSICLOAD](0B5_MUSICLOAD).

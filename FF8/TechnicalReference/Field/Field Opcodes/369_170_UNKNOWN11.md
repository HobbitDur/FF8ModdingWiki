-   Opcode: **0x170**
-   Short name: **UNKNOWN11**
-   Long name: Turn to party character

#### Argument

none

#### Stack

  
*Party character (0-2)*

*Turn speed*

**UNKNOWN11**

#### Description

Starts turning this entity to face the field entity of the given party member (resolved through the savemap party→entity mapping), taking the shortest rotation path. The turn runs asynchronously; use [UNKNOWN12](../171-unknown12/) to wait for completion.

PC handler: `SCRIPT_UNKNOWN11` at 0x5279B0 (renamed `SCRIPT_UNKNOWN11_turn_to_party_char` in the community IDB).
